This was the "capstone" project from all the course's classes that went about Web-Scrapping. It involved BeautifulSoup and Selenium, and the main goal was to automate a data entry job.

The supposed position involved a research through a rental site, from which we needed to extract the price, address and link from each available rental in San Francisco, CA.
It was a good practice for making BS and Selenium interact in a functional way.
